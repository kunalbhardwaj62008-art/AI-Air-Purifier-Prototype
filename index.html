🧊 AI Smart Air Purifier

Arduino-Powered Precision Air Quality Control for Healthier Living

🚀 Product Overview

Experience professional-grade air purification with our AI Smart Air Purifier. Powered by Arduino Uno and advanced MQ135 sensor technology, it intelligently monitors and cleans indoor air by detecting pollutants like CO2, smoke, VOCs, and more. Dual HEPA + Carbon filtration removes 99.97% of 0.3μm particles and odors, covering up to 200 sq ft effectively.[web:24][web:29]

Real-time AQI display, color-coded alerts, and automatic fan activation ensure your air stays clean 24/7. Compact, energy-efficient (12V operation), and fully customizable.

✨ Key Features

📊

Real-Time AQI Monitoring

MQ135 sensor samples air every second, calculates AQI (0-500), displayed on OLED screen.

💨

Auto Fan Activation

120mm DC fan + relay turns ON automatically when AQI > 100. Air flows through HEPA + Carbon filters.

🎨

Visual Status Indicators

RGB LED: Green (Good), Yellow (Moderate), Red (Poor). Buzzer alerts at AQI > 150.

📱

OLED Dashboard

0.96" display shows AQI, raw PPM, fan status, and pollution level in real-time.

🔧

Smart Calibration

Self-adjusts to baseline clean air. PWM fan speed scales with pollution severity.

⚡

Energy Efficient

12V adapter + buck converter. Sleep mode when AQI < 30. Low power consumption (~5W max).

🧹 Maintenance & Safety

  • Replace HEPA/Carbon filters every 6-12 months (or 3000 hours).[web:21][web:24]
  • Clean pre-filter monthly with vacuum.
  • Calibrate MQ135 in clean outdoor air annually.
  • Operate in 10-35°C, away from direct sunlight.
  • Power: 12V DC 2A adapter only. Buck converter handles 5V logic.
  • Safety: Relay isolates high-voltage fan circuit.
